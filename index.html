About ForPenSec

ForPenSec Consulting & Technologies is a specialist cybersecurity firm based in Pondicherry, India. We were founded with a clear mission — to make enterprise-grade security accessible to businesses of all sizes.

Led by K. Pradeep Kumar, a Digital Forensics and Cybersecurity Consultant, ForPenSec combines deep technical expertise with a practical, business-first approach to security. We don't just find problems — we help you fix them.

Whether you are a small business looking to secure your network, or an organisation that has experienced a cyber incident and needs expert investigation, ForPenSec has the skills and experience to help.
